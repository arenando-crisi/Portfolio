Pivó - StreetBasket community app

Pivó is a branding and interaction design project linked to the world of street basketball.
It is an application that has two souls, the first is the more organizational one, thus allowing you to organize matches and events within the streetbasket courts in Milan, with a tool that allows you to see the status of the court and choose the difficulty of game.
The second soul of this project wants to place this app at the center of the game of street basketball allowing, through the sharing of contents related to the pitch, to be the center of the community of pitches. The branding is therefore placed in the middle of these two worlds, with clean graphics, dirtied by street elements such as graffiti.
My role was about web design and development.
