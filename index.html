Pivó
Pivó is a branding and interaction design project carried out within the interactio design lab of the Communication Design Course at Politecnico di Milano. It is a project of an application to meet streetbasket players and provide them an aggregation and organizational tool. My role was web design and development, as well as being part of the branding and copywriting team.

Lineo
Lineo is a speculative design project that investigates the possibility of altering people's future by modifying the lines present on their hands. The physical part of the project, centered on the prototype, accommodates the user's hand and controls the virtual interface through motorized sliders and the Aduino microcontroller. The virtual part consists of the website and an application to support the prototype. The branding draws inspiration from the dualism of the nature of the project, based in fact on medicine and palmistry, the basic element is the circle that also defines the shape of the prototype.
Anno: 2020
LINEO
Milano da bere
"Milano da bere" is a graphic design of a deck of poker cards with a Milanese alcohol theme, specifically Campari, Amaro Ramazzotti, Fernet Branca and Gin Rosa. The illustrations are made with the watercolor technique. The cover features a pattern reminiscent of the shape of typical industry goblets. All the illustrations are themed and cover various aspects of the drinks in question, including cocktails recipes as figures and tools as jokers.
Anno: 2018

THIS IS ME!!




Hi! My name is Andrea Corsini and I am a budding designer.
I live in a small town that curiously enough is exactly halfway between the cities of Lecco and Bergamo. Once I finished high school I discovered what it meant to be a designer, so I decided to enroll in the Faculty of Communication Design at the Politecnico di Milano, finally finding a stimulating and creative environment.
I like to define the work of the designer as that of someone who, after understanding how disparate things work, is able to design new things that encapsulate his or her experience to put at the service of the community to help address everyday problems. On the graphics and communication side, I can't say I have a defined style but, I like to work with 3D graphics, sans serif typography and strong color contrasts.
Finally, a passion of mine is everything around the maker sphere, especially rapid prototyping platforms like Arduino.
