AG Fronzoni
Utopia e Rivoluzione in pochi segni

This project was born within the editorial framework of the DesignVerso project, a publication which, through articles and interviews, aims to reconstruct the figure of important communication designers.
In particular, this is dedicated to AG Fronzoni, an important Tuscan designer who often and willingly worked in Milan collaborating with major institutions and artists such as Lucio Fontana.
The elements of Fronzoni's style have been recalled on the cover and in the textual inserts, such as black and white, the text inclination and the sans serif typography. The project brief also required an insert that could be interactive and linked to the work of the designer, here it is a magnetic board to which you can attach magnetic characters to compose a poster, similarly to how Fronzoni made up his own.
Lastly, the bookmark takes up the poster designed for the Lucio Fontana exhibition, perhaps one of the most important works by AG Fronzoni.
